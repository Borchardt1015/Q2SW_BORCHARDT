5 Friendly Dog Breeds

Golden Retrievers The Golden Retriever is a sturdy, muscular dog of medium size, famous for the dense, lustrous coat of gold that gives the breed its name. The broad head, with its friendly and intelligent eyes, short ears, and straight muzzle, is a breed hallmark. Height: (Male): 23-24 inches (Female): 21.5-22.5 inches Weight: (Male): 65-75 lb (Female): 55-65 lb Life Expectancy:10-12 Years
Shih Tzu The Shih Tzu is known to be especially affectionate with children. As a small dog bred to spend most of their day inside royal palaces, they make a great pet if you live in an apartment or lack a big backyard. Height:(Male&Female) 9-10.5 inches Weight: (Male&Female) 9-16 lb Life Expectancy:10-18 Years
German Shepherd German Shepherd Dog is a large, agile, muscular dog of noble character and high intelligence. Loyal, confident, courageous, and steady, the German Shepherd is truly a dog lover's delight. German Shepherd Dogs can stand as high as 26 inches at the shoulder and, when viewed in outline, presents a picture of smooth, graceful curves rather than angles. Height: (Male): 24-26 inches (Female): 22-24 inches Weight: (Male): 65-90 lb (Female): 50-70 lb Life Expectancy: 7-10 Years
American Bulldog American Bulldogs are a well-balanced athletic dog that demonstrate great strength, endurance, agility, and a friendly attitude. Historically, they were bred to be a utility dog used for working the farm. Height: (Male): 22-25 inches (Female): 20-23 inches Weight: (Male): 75-100 lb (Female): 60-80 lb Life Expectancy: 10-12 Years
Cardigan Welsh Corgi Cardigans are powerful workers of deceptive speed and grace. Cardis can weigh anywhere from 25 to 34 pounds, with females at the lower end of the scale. They come in several coat colors, from red to the popular blue-merle pattern. Height:(Male&Female) 10.5-12.5 inches Weight: (Male): 30-38 lb (Female): 25-34 lb Life Expectancy: 12-15 Years
